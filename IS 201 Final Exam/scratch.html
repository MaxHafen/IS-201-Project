Steps to having a great BBQ

  1. Quality Food
    • You need to get good quality meat and get a lot of it.
    • Have plenty of sauces and seasonings to help amplify the flavor.
    • Have many great sides to go with your bbq like rolls, corn, beans, or assorted fruits
  2. Quality Location and Equipment
    • Pick a place where you can host many people and have a large eating area.
    • Make sure you have a good grill and all of your grilling utensils.
    • Check the weather to make sure it will not rain.
  3. Quality People
    • Invite friends and family in advance of the BBQ.
    • Have people bring side dishes to help improve the BBQ.
    • Bring yard games to help entertain your guests.
